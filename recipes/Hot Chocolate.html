Hot Chocolate

Description

This is a recipe to make a classic drink for a cold day

This Recipe only requires four ingredients and is very simple to make

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Marshmellows

Directions

  1. Begin by heating milk in a small saucepan
  2. Once heated, pore milk into mug and add cocoa powder and sugar
  3. Stir until all ingredients are mixed in and disolved
  4. Add marshmellows and enjoy
