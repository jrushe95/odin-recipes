Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

This is a recipe to make a classic peanut butter and jelly sandwhich.

You can use this recipe as a baseline to make other variations of a classic sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Jelly
  • Peanut Butter (Crunchy or Smooth)

Directions

  1. Place two slices of bread on a plate
  2. Spoon Jelly onto one of the slices of bread and use the knife to spread it around.
  3. Clean the knife and use it to spread peanut butter on the other slice of bread.
  4. Place the two prepared slices of bread on top of eachother so that the jelly and peanut butter are touching
  5. Slice sandwich into to pieces and enjoy.
